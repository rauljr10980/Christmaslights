Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free estimates?

Not sure exactly what you want to be done? Take advantage of our free, no-obligation estimate to get recommendations from our experts on how to transform your property through outdoor lighting.

When is the earliest you can get my lights up?

Like to plan ahead? We get it! We can get your lights up in early October so that's one less thing you have to worry about this holiday season. Scheduling your appointment early will also make your life easier, as our slots tend to fill up quickly as the holidays approach.

What if my timer goes out, or I experience another issue after the installation?

Many of us have had the experience of putting up lights, only to hit a snag when things aren't working as they should later on in the season. Even worse, this always seems to happen at the most inconvenient time when holiday stresses have hit their peak. That's why many of our customers appreciate that our technicians will return for any needed maintenance so that any needed adjustments can be made quickly. Whether it's a bulb that needs to be replaced or a timer that is off, you can have peace of mind knowing that you can reach out to us and we will send someone back out, at no additional cost to you.

What happens if my lights stop working?

Our lighting pros perform multiple checks on all the equipment we install to make sure everything is working before we complete our installation visit. In the unlikely event of inclement weather or any other type of problem that might cause some type of electrical problem which could impact your display, we will return to make any needed repairs.
